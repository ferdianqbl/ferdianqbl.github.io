What places can you visit ?

Borobudur Temple

Borobudur Temple was built in 824 AD by King Samaratungga during the Syailendra dynasty. The construction of Borobudur Temple was completed in 847 AD by Ratu Prabudawardhani, daughter of Samaratungga to glorify Mahayana Buddhism.

Borobudur Temple is located in Borobudur Village, Borobudura District, Magelang Regency, Central Java with an area of 123 x 123 square meters consisting of 504 Buddha statues, 72 overlay stupas, and 1 main stupa.

Nepal Van Java

Located at an altitude of 1600 meters above sea level, making Butuh or Mangli Village the highest village located in Magelang Regency, Central Java. With this altitude, this area has a cool, beautiful, fresh atmosphere, and beautiful scenery.

In the past, this village was just a hiking trail to Mount Sumbing. Until 2019, this village was transformed into a famous tourist spot with colorfully painted houses and stacked locations. It brings a new atmosphere, like the village of Namche Bazaar in Nepal which is located in the Himalayas. Until the creation of Nepal Van Java which is popular on social media because it is very instagrammable and contemporary.

Other Tourist Spots

Kyai Langgeng Park

Kyai Langgeng Park is one of the natural attractions and games in Magelang City. You can also do outbound here.

Mangli Tourism Village

Mangli, which is located at an altitude of 1,570 meters above sea level, provides natural views of mountains, forests, waterfalls, vegetable agribusiness, and animal conservation.
